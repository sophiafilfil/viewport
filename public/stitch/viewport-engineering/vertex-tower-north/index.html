Structural Integrity & Vision

Vertex Tower North represents a paradigm shift in high-rise engineering. Designed to withstand extreme wind loads and seismic activity common in the Pacific Northwest, the tower utilizes a revolutionary **Central Composite Core** integrated with an external **Steel Exoskeleton**.

Our team implemented a data-driven approach, utilizing real-time sensor feedback during the construction phase to optimize load distribution across the podium levels. The result is a structure that is 15% lighter than conventional skyscrapers of similar height while maintaining superior rigidity.
